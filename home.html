About Us

Welcome to PCTE Guest Lectures & Industrial Visits

Since 1999, the PCTE Group of Institutes, nestled in the culturally vibrant state of Punjab, India, has been expertly cultivating future leaders. We provide a comprehensive educational experience, honing both practical skills and theoretical knowledge across a variety of disciplines.

PCTE, a proud affiliate of the IK Gujral Punjab Technical University, is acclaimed for its enriching cultural events and co-curricular activities such as Koshish, Ehsaas, Turf, and more. These monthly events offer students invaluable opportunities to engage, learn and grow beyond the traditional classroom setting
